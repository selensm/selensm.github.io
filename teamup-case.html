Portfolio > TeamUp! App Concept

This concept aims to bring people who want to play sports together. It helps people find or create teams that anyone can join and encourages building a community around a shared interest - playing sports.

My role in the project

UX Research, UX / UI Design

The concept

TeamUp! is a native mobile app that helps people form, join, and maintain groups to play different sports with. For long-term users it doubles as a social media app where they can chat with friends they have within the app or in group chats that automatically get created when a sports team is created; they can also keep track of different events to join with their teams.

TeamUp! offers opportunities to customize the experience and allows users to be able to distinguish between different types of groups and group dynamics so they can choose the best group for themselves.

The process

The assignment me and my team dealt with during this project was to create a concept that would help people with a common interest form groups. As this was kind of a broad "wicked" problem, the team decided to use Design Thinking as a framework.

After doing user research and analysing that through empathy mapping, the team outlined three main problems to be addressed: 1) people struggle to form groups because they don't want to take an initiative; 2) they struggle to form groups because it is hard to find others with the same mindset; 3) there isn't a platform that would make it easy to do so. Additionally, there were a lot of people who also preferred forming or joining groups with at least on person they know.

Bringing value to the end user

To bring value through this concept meant to address all of the aforementioned problems. Therefore the app contains features like - setting your preferences as you sign up, joining groups, forming groups and inviting friends to groups (in-app or Facebook friends).

TeamUp! not only helps people find a likeminded community with a shared interest but provides everything users might need to maintain that community - chat, evenets.

The app removes the social anxiety barrier for it's users by providing the opportunity to form their own group with their friends so they feel safe and less intimidated to meet new people through the app.

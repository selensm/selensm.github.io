Portfolio > Central Car Wash Website & Webshop

The goal for CCW was to attract more people, have a great brand identity and express better it through their website and have a high standard of usability since their communication with their clients was going through their current website.

My role in the project

UX Design, UI Design

Tools and technologies

Adobe XD, Adobe Illustrator

The challenge

Since the client already had a team of developers who would continue working on the website after the end of the project, it was necessary to communicate in the best way possible how things should be implemented both design and code-wise. So the team decided to make a UI kit specifically for the company for their developers to follow in the future. As the UI designer, I had to consider all basic elements and create clear guidelines that would not create any confusions. In order to be able to solve this, team made a decision to work with BootStrap both in the UI kit and in the code since it was a widely known library.
